Biryani

Biryani is one of the most popular dishes in South Asia, as well as among the diaspora from the region.

Similar dishes are also prepared in other parts of the world such as in Iraq, Thailand, and Malaysia.

Biryani is the single most-ordered dish on Indian online food ordering and delivery services.

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Eggs
  • Spice
  • Vegetables
  • Rice

Steps

  1. Heat a heavy-bottomed pan with a tight-fitting lid on medium-low.
  2. Cover the bottom of the pan with water or stock.
  3. Place the biryani in the heated pan and cover with a lid.
  4. On medium-low heat, steam for 5-6 minutes.
  5. Stir occasionally to distribute the heat evenly.
